<p><p>Transformers for frequency converters are crucial in adapting electrical power for various industrial applications. The market is primarily divided into dry type and oil immersed transformers. Dry type transformers use air for cooling, making them ideal for indoor installations with limited space and lower maintenance. In contrast, oil immersed transformers rely on oil for cooling and insulation, providing efficient heat dissipation and higher capacity for outdoor applications. Both types play essential roles in enhancing energy efficiency and performance in power conversion systems.</p></p>
