<p><p>The anti-rotation boot market is divided into two main types: walking boots and non-walking boots. Walking boots are designed to allow ambulatory movement while providing support and protection for injuries or post-surgery recovery. They typically feature a rigid structure and cushioned sole. In contrast, non-walking boots immobilize the foot completely, preventing any weight-bearing activity while facilitating healing. Both types serve to enhance patient recovery, though their usage varies based on the severity of the injury and the mobility needs of the patient.</p></p>
