<p><strong>The GaN Epitaxial Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CVD</li><li>MBE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GaN epitaxial market primarily encompasses two types of growth techniques: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE). CVD involves the deposition of gaseous precursors onto a substrate, leading to the formation of high-quality films suitable for various applications. In contrast, MBE utilizes beams of molecules to create layers in a vacuum, ensuring precise control over thickness and composition. Both methods are crucial for advancing GaN-based devices, contributing to innovations in power electronics and optoelectronics.</p></p>
