<p><ul><li>Water Soluble</li><li>Oil Soluble</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water-resistant film former market is divided into two primary types: water-soluble and oil-soluble. Water-soluble film formers dissolve in water, creating a protective barrier that maintains hydration while enabling easy rinse-off. They are often used in personal care products such as hair styling gels and moisturizers. In contrast, oil-soluble film formers are resistant to water and typically used in products that require longer-lasting protection, such as waterproof cosmetics and sunscreens. Both types cater to distinct applications and consumer preferences.</p></p>
