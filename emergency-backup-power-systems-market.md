<p><p>The emergency backup power systems market includes Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS) and backup generators. UPS systems provide instantaneous power during outages, ensuring devices remain active without interruption, making them ideal for sensitive electronics and critical applications. Backup generators, on the other hand, supply power for extended periods during a blackout by using fuel sources like diesel or natural gas. Together, these systems enhance resilience against power disruptions across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
