<p><p>The mineral fiber acoustic panel market comprises various types of materials used for sound absorption and insulation. Glasswool is lightweight and offers excellent thermal properties. Stonewool provides fire resistance and soundproofing. Rockwool, made from volcanic rock, offers durability and moisture resistance. Slagwool utilizes industrial by-products for insulation. Glass filaments include fine fibers for enhanced acoustics. Ceramic fibers withstand high temperatures, making them ideal for specialized applications. Each type contributes unique benefits to sound management and thermal efficiency in construction and design.</p></p>
