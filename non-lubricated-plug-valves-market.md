<p><p>Non-lubricated plug valves are essential components in various industrial applications, providing reliable flow control for liquids, gases, and slurries. These valves operate using a cylindrical or tapered plug that rotates within the valve body, allowing for on/off control or throttling. Unlike lubricated plug valves, non-lubricated variants do not require additional lubrication, which simplifies maintenance and reduces contamination risks in sensitive processes.</p><p>The Non-lubricated Plug Valves Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, driven by increasing demand in sectors such as oil and gas, water and wastewater treatment, and chemicals.
