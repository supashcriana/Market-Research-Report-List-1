Conductive plastic potentiometers offer high accuracy and smooth operation, making them ideal for precision applications. Cermet potentiometers combine ceramic and metal materials, providing durability and stability in harsh environments. Each type serves distinct functionalities based on performance, environmental resilience, and application needs.</p></p>
