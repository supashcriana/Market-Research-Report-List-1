<p><p>The Human Rabies Vaccines Market is primarily divided into two applications: Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis (PrEP) and Post-Exposure Prophylaxis (PEP). PrEP is administered to individuals at high risk of rabies exposure, such as veterinarians or travelers to endemic areas, enhancing their immunity before potential contact. PEP is given after suspected exposure to rabies, involving a series of vaccines to prevent the onset of the disease. Both applications are vital for controlling rabies and safeguarding public health through effective immunization strategies.</p></p>
