<p><p>The heat seal tapes market comprises three main types: PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) Type, TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) Type, and PI (Polyimide) Type. PTFE tapes offer excellent thermal resistance and non-stick properties, making them ideal for applications in high-temperature environments. TPU tapes provide flexibility, durability, and a strong seal, suitable for diverse industrial applications. PI tapes are renowned for their high-temperature stability and chemical resistance, serving in electronics and aerospace sectors. Each type caters to specific performance requirements.</p></p>
