<p><ul><li>Slow freezing</li><li>Vitrification</li><li>Ultra-rapid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cryonics technology encompasses three primary market types for preserving human remains at ultra-low temperatures: </p><p>1. **Slow Freezing** involves gradually lowering temperatures to minimize ice crystal formation, which can damage cells. </p><p>2. **Vitrification** employs chemical solutions to convert biological tissues into a glass-like state, preventing ice formation entirely. </p><p>3. **Ultra-Rapid Freezing** rapidly freezes tissues to halt decay instantly, aiming to minimize cellular damage. Each method reflects a unique approach to preservation, impacting viability and the potential for a future revival.</p></p>
