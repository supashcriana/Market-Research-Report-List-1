<p><p>The blood purification consumable market encompasses various types of materials used in medical procedures. Hemodialysis materials facilitate waste and toxin removal from the blood through an artificial kidney. Peritoneal dialysis materials use the patient's abdominal cavity for filtration. Plasmapheresis materials are designed for separating plasma from blood, typically for therapeutic purposes. Continuous blood purification materials are used in critical care settings for ongoing filtration, while "Others" includes additional products not classified in these categories, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
