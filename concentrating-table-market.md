<p><p>Concentrating tables are used in mineral processing to separate heavy minerals from lighter ones based on specific gravity. The market types include single deck, double deck, and triple deck tables. A single deck table features one surface for separation, suitable for simpler applications. The double deck table has two surfaces, allowing for more efficient processing and higher throughput. A triple deck table incorporates three separation surfaces, maximizing recovery by processing more material and providing enhanced sorting capabilities for complex ores.</p></p>
