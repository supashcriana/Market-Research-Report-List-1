<p><ul><li>Active Soil Depressurization (Interior)</li><li>Active Soil Depressurization (Exterior)</li><li>Active Soil Depressurization (Slab)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Whole-House Radon Mitigation System market encompasses various techniques to reduce radon levels in homes. Active Soil Depressurization (Interior) involves installing a fan and suction system within the building, drawing radon from beneath the foundation into the atmosphere. Active Soil Depressurization (Exterior) employs a similar method but places the fan outside, minimizing noise and improving aesthetics. Active Soil Depressurization (Slab) targets homes with concrete slabs, using a system designed to penetrate the slab and effectively manage radon levels.</p></p>
