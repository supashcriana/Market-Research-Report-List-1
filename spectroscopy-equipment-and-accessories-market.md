<p><p>The spectroscopy equipment and accessories market includes various types such as molecular spectrometers, atomic spectrometers, and mass spectrometers. Molecular spectrometers analyze the interaction of light with molecules, providing insights into molecular structure and composition. Atomic spectrometers focus on the elemental composition of samples by measuring light emitted or absorbed by atoms. Mass spectrometers identify and quantify compounds based on their mass-to-charge ratio, enabling detailed analysis of complex mixtures. Together, these instruments are essential in research, quality control, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
