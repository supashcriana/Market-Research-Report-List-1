<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC sensor device market comprises various technologies, primarily focusing on two types: PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID sensors utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules, offering high sensitivity to volatile organic compounds across a wide range. Conversely, MOS sensors detect VOCs through changes in electrical conductivity when gases adsorb onto heated metal oxides. Both types serve crucial roles in environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and indoor air quality management, catering to diverse applications in various industries.</p></p>
