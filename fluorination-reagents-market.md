<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fluorination Reagents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrophilic Fluorination Reagents</li><li>Nucleophilic Fluorination Reagents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluorination reagents market is categorized into two main types: electrophilic and nucleophilic fluorination reagents. Electrophilic fluorination reagents are typically used to introduce fluorine into organic substrates by acting as electron-deficient species, facilitating the incorporation of fluorine atoms into molecules. In contrast, nucleophilic fluorination reagents contain electron-rich fluorine atoms that can displace other functional groups in substrates, allowing for the addition of fluorine through nucleophilic attack. Both types are essential for various applications in pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, and fluorinated materials.</p></p>
