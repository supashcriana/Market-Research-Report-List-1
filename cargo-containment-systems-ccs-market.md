<p><p>Cargo Containment Systems (CCS) are essential for safely transporting liquefied gases. The Membrane Type CCS utilizes a thin, flexible membrane to contain the cargo, often allowing for lighter and more efficient designs. In contrast, the Independent Type CCS comprises distinct, rigid tanks that can withstand cargo pressure independently. Both systems cater to different operational needs and regulatory standards within the maritime industry, contributing to safe and efficient liquid gas transport across various sectors, including energy and chemical industries.</p></p>
