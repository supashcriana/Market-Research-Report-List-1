<p><p>The containment barrier market is divided into two main types: rigid and semi-rigid barriers. Rigid barriers are strong, inflexible structures designed to fully contain hazardous materials, providing maximum protection in critical environments. In contrast, semi-rigid barriers offer a balance between flexibility and strength, allowing for some adaptability while still ensuring efficient containment. Both types are vital in industries such as chemical processing and oil and gas, where managing spills and leaks is essential for safety and environmental protection.</p></p>
