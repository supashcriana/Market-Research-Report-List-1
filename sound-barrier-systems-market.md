<p><strong>The Sound Barrier Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective</li><li>Absorptive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sound barrier systems are categorized into two main types: reflective and absorptive. Reflective barriers, typically made of hard materials, deflect sound waves back towards the source, effectively blocking noise from traveling into sensitive areas. In contrast, absorptive barriers are designed to absorb sound energy, reducing echo and sound transmission by using porous materials. Both types are essential in managing noise pollution around highways, industrial sites, and urban environments, contributing to improved acoustic comfort and environmental quality.</p></p>
