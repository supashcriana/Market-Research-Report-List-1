<p><p>The 48V DC-DC converter market is divided into two main types: isolated and non-isolated converters. Isolated converters utilize transformer coupling to separate input and output, providing enhanced safety and noise immunity, making them suitable for sensitive applications like telecom and industrial systems. Non-isolated converters, on the other hand, directly connect input and output, offering compact designs and higher efficiency for applications such as consumer electronics and automotive systems. Each type serves specific needs based on performance, safety, and application requirements.</p></p>
