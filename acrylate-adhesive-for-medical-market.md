<p><p>Acrylate adhesives are versatile bonding agents utilized extensively in the medical market due to their strong adhesion and biocompatibility. They come in two main types: general acrylates, which are flexible and suitable for various applications, and cyanoacrylates, known for their rapid curing and strong bond strength. Cyanoacrylates are particularly useful for surgical procedures and wound closures, while acrylates support a broader range of medical devices, offering durability and resistance to environmental factors. Both types ensure reliable performance in critical medical applications.</p></p>
