<p><ul><li>Isolated</li><li>Non-Isolated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The closed-loop current sensor market is divided into two main types: isolated and non-isolated sensors. Isolated current sensors provide electrical separation between the sensor and the monitored circuit, ensuring safety and reducing the risk of interference. They are ideal for high-voltage applications. In contrast, non-isolated sensors do not offer this separation, making them more compact and cost-effective, suitable for lower voltage applications. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, including automotive, renewable energy, and industrial automation.</p></p>
