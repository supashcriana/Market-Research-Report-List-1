<p><p>The Swing Check Valves market is segmented into two primary connection types: flanged and threaded connections. Flanged connection valves utilize a flange at each end for secure attachment to piping systems, offering robust sealing and ease of maintenance, ideal for larger pipelines. Conversely, threaded connection valves feature male or female threads for direct screwing into pipes, providing a compact design suitable for smaller applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, ensuring efficient fluid flow while preventing backflow.</p></p>
