<p><p>The soil enzyme market comprises various enzyme types, each serving distinct roles in soil health and fertility. Hydrolytic enzymes break down complex organic materials into simpler compounds, enhancing nutrient availability. Oxidoreductases facilitate redox reactions, playing a key role in organic matter decomposition and nutrient cycling. Transferases are involved in transferring functional groups, aiding in the modification of organic substrates. Lyases catalyze the addition or removal of groups to form double bonds, contributing to metabolic processes. Together, these enzymes enhance soil productivity and ecosystem sustainability.</p></p>
