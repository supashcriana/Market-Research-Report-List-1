<p><p>The sports and energy drinks market comprises two main categories: general energy drinks and energy shots. General energy drinks typically feature larger serving sizes and are designed for prolonged energy boosts, often containing caffeine, sugars, and additional performance-enhancing ingredients. Energy shots, on the other hand, are smaller, concentrated liquid servings meant for quick energy boosts, usually with high caffeine content but minimal sugars. Both types cater to different consumer needs, from endurance athletes to those seeking immediate alertness.</p></p>
