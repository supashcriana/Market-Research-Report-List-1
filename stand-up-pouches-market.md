<p><p>The stand-up pouches market features several types tailored for different packaging needs. Aseptic pouches are designed for long shelf life without refrigeration, ideal for liquids. Standard pouches are versatile and commonly used for snacks and dry goods. Retort pouches are heat-resistant and suitable for pre-cooked meals, allowing for extended shelf stability. Hot-filled pouches utilize a filling process that preserves the product's integrity and freshness by sealing while the contents are hot. Each type serves specific product requirements and consumer demands.</p></p>
