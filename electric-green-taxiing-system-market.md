<p><strong>The Electric Green Taxiing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-Board Type</li><li>External Systems Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Green Taxiing System (eTaxi) market comprises two main types: On-Board Type and External Systems Type. The On-Board Type integrates electric taxiing mechanisms directly within the aircraft, reducing reliance on conventional engines during ground operations, enhancing fuel efficiency, and minimizing emissions. Conversely, the External Systems Type involves ground-based electric systems that assist aircraft in taxiing without using onboard engines. Both types aim to improve airport efficiency, reduce environmental impact, and promote sustainable aviation practices.</p></p>
