<p><p>The PPE/PS alloy market is primarily segmented into two types: PPE/HIPS (High Impact Polystyrene) and PPE/GPPS (General Purpose Polystyrene). PPE/HIPS combines the high impact strength of HIPS with the thermal stability of PPE, making it suitable for applications requiring durability and toughness, such as automotive components and electronic housings. Conversely, PPE/GPPS blends offer enhanced rigidity and clarity, ideal for packaging and consumer goods. Both types leverage the beneficial properties of their components to cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
