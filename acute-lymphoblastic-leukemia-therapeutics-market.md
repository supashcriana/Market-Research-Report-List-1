<p><p>The Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia (ALL) therapeutics market includes several treatment types. Chemotherapy is the primary approach, using powerful drugs to kill cancer cells. Targeted therapy focuses on specific genetic mutations in leukemia cells, minimizing damage to healthy tissues. Radiation therapy employs high-energy rays to eradicate remaining cancer cells, especially in the central nervous system. Stem cell transplantation replaces damaged bone marrow with healthy cells, enhancing recovery. These various treatment modalities provide comprehensive options for managing ALL effectively.</p></p>
