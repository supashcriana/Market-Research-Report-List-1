<p><p>The Portable High Voltage Probe market is categorized into two main types: active and passive probes. Active probes incorporate electronic components that amplify signals, providing higher accuracy and wider bandwidth, making them suitable for precise measurements in complex environments. In contrast, passive probes rely solely on resistive and capacitive elements, offering simplicity and reliability, but with limited bandwidth and sensitivity. Both types serve essential roles in electrical testing, diagnostics, and research, catering to different measurement needs based on specific applications.</p></p>
