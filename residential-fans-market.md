<p><p>The residential fans market comprises two main types: AC (Alternating Current) residential fans and DC (Direct Current) residential fans. AC fans are traditionally used, powered by household electricity, offering reliable performance and cost-effectiveness. They typically consume more energy. In contrast, DC fans are more energy-efficient, quieter, and often come with variable speed settings, making them increasingly popular among eco-conscious consumers. The choice between AC and DC fans depends on factors like energy consumption, cost, and specific user preferences.</p></p>
