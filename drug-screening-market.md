Rapid testing devices offer quick results, often in point-of-care settings, while assay kits and reagents provide necessary tools for laboratory testing, catering to the needs of clinical, forensic, and workplace drug testing applications. Together, they enhance drug detection efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
