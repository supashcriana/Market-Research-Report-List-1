<p><ul><li>Straight Silicone Fluid</li><li>Modified Silicone Fluid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial silicone fluid market is primarily categorized into two types: straight silicone fluids and modified silicone fluids. Straight silicone fluids are composed of linear polydimethylsiloxane, known for their stability, low toxicity, and thermal resistance, making them ideal for various applications like lubricants and hydraulic fluids. Modified silicone fluids, on the other hand, are chemically altered to enhance specific properties such as surface tension and compatibility, making them suitable for uses in coatings, adhesives, and personal care products.</p></p>
