<p><p>The EUV pellicle market is segmented based on transmittance levels, which determine the effectiveness of light transmission in extreme ultraviolet lithography. Pellicles with ≥90% transmittance are optimal for high-resolution patterns, ensuring minimal impact on imaging. The 85%-90% range offers a balance between protection and transmission efficiency. Pellicles in the 80%-85% category may compromise some resolution but are more cost-effective. Those with ≤80% transmittance are typically less desirable due to significant interference with light, affecting performance.</p></p>
