<p><p>The COF (Coefficient of Friction) Tester market can be categorized into dynamic and static types. Dynamic COF testers measure friction during material movement, providing insights into how surfaces behave under motion, crucial for industries like automotive and packaging. In contrast, static COF testers assess friction when materials are at rest, helping determine adhesion and grip properties. Both types are essential for quality control and product safety, ensuring that materials meet specific performance criteria in various applications.</p></p>
