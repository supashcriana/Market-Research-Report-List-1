<p><p>The bicycle stems market includes three main types: threadless stems, adjustable stems, and quill stems. Threadless stems offer a modern, lightweight design with a clean look, allowing for easy adjustments and better stiffness. Adjustable stems provide versatility with adjustable angles, catering to riders seeking customizable comfort. Quill stems, typically seen in older bicycles, feature a more traditional design that allows for height adjustment via a wedge mechanism. Each type caters to different riding styles and preferences, enhancing overall cycling experience.</p></p>
