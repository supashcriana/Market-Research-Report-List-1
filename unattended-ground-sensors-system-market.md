<p><p>The Unattended Ground Sensors (UGS) market includes various sensor types designed for surveillance and monitoring. Seismic UGS detects ground vibrations caused by movement, providing valuable information on potential threats. Acoustic UGS captures sound waves to identify noises associated with activities such as footsteps or vehicles. Magnetic UGS senses disturbances in the Earth's magnetic field, useful for detecting metallic objects. Infrared UGS utilizes thermal imaging to spot heat signatures, particularly in low visibility. Other sensors may encompass visual, chemical, or multisensor capabilities for comprehensive monitoring.</p></p>
