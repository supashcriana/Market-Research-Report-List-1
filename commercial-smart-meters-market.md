<p><p>The commercial smart meters market consists of single-phase and three-phase smart meters, tailored for different electrical systems. Single-phase smart meters are designed for residential and small commercial use, measuring electricity consumption in simple electrical setups. In contrast, three-phase smart meters are used in larger commercial and industrial applications, offering greater capacity to monitor energy usage across more complex electrical networks. Both types enhance energy management, improve billing accuracy, and facilitate real-time monitoring for utilities and consumers alike.</p></p>
